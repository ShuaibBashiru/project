BRIEF HISTORY OF YABATECH MUSLIM COMMUNITY

Yaba College of Technology was founded in the year 1947 with the objective of educating the society in the area of technical-education. However, other curricular activities such as games, sports and religious activities were later introduced because they were, and are still, part of education. Those activities that support education provided the ample opportunity for Muslim staff and students to associate with a view to pursuing common religious goals, such as observance of daily Solawat and Jumat service.
